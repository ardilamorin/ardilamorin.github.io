My Philosophy

My Leadership philosophy is based on three pillars that, when combined, create an environment where design thrives.

01. Influence with data & insights

I gain influence, instead of authority, using research and data to validate design's strategic value, providing the evidence that drives alignment across teams, and gathering the insights that inform roadmaps.

02. Enable scale through systems

I provide value, rather than restrictions, is the best way to scale good design. I use processes, systems and tools that empower product teams to their best work. raising the bar for speed and quality.

03. Drive change through collaboration

I create sustainable change by establishing relationships that build trust. Constantly delivering good quality, setting and managing expectations, and becoming a true partner that can answer to business and user needs.
